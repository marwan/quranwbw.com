About
Quranwbw.com is a word-by-word Quran learning website where you can see the translation and transliteration for each word and also listen the audio for each word and each verse.

Quranwbw.com wouldn't be possible without these amazing websites:
  • Dr. Shehnaz Shaikh - for her amazing work on the word-by-word compilation.
  • quran.com - for their amazing API and word-by-word audio files.
  • tanzil.net - for their wonderful collection of Quranic resources.
  • seventysixnine.com - for their initial words database.
  • everyayah.com & qurancentral.com - for their amazing collection of Quranic audios.
If you have any suggestions, or found a bug, or would like to contribute, then please contact me on my email at quranwbw@gmail.com. You can also find the words API for development at api.quranwbw.com
